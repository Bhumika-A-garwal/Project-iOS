All options on table to secure Covid vaccines


NEW DELHI:India is exploring all options - Including provisions under the Essential Commodities Act, Drugs and Cosmetics Act and Disaster Act - to secure adequate stocks as well as bargain for a reasonable pricing of Covid shots, even os the large-scale domestic vaccine manufacturing capacities provide it a strong edge to negotiate with companies. For instance Essential Commodities Act's Section 3 gives the Central powers to control production supply, distribution etc of essential commodities which Includes vaccines. The provision also empowers the government to ask firms to sell whole or specified part of the quantity to central or state governments, It required.
